CAREERS AT SWEET TREETS

Come join us on our mission of making every day a bit sweeter!
CAKE DECORATOR/ARTIST

Our cake artist team works in a dynamic environment and are required to ensure product quality and consistency in order to meet the needs of every customer and our high standards.

All experience levels are welcome to apply. Experience in: piping, building, stacking, buttercream frosting, fondant work, and wedding cakes is considered advantageous.

 KITCHEN MANAGER/HEAD BAKER

This is a rare and exciting opportunity for someone with management experience to join an established, high-quality, and critically acclaimed cake bakery. Ideal candidate has 3-5 years experience working in high-volume wholesale, daily bakery and/or cake bakery w/ 1-2 years of management experience. Help us maintain our reputation and continue to grow our bakery to bring more delicious goods to Austin.
